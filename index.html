Overview

Charminar is an iconic 16th-century mosque with 4 grand arches and 48-meter tall minarets. Located in the heart of Hyderabad's old city, it stands as a testament to the architectural brilliance of the Mughal era. The monument offers spectacular views over the famous Laad Bazaar and is one of India's most recognizable landmarks.
